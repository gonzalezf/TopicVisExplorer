To split a topic, you must select the most relevant documents to the new subtopics. For each category, you may choose as many documents as you deem necessary. Documents in neither subtopic will be categorized automatically.
  • You must place at least one document in new subtopic A and at least one in new subtopic B (neither is not enough).
  • Use the table pagination to reach more documents if the ones you need are on another page.
  • The counts in the footer update as you choose A / B / neither for each row.

How does splitting work?

Each row is one document. Pick exactly one of A, B, or “neither” per row. The model uses your A and B choices as seed documents for the two new subtopics. Documents you leave on “neither” are still split automatically, but you must still seed both subtopics with at least one document each.
